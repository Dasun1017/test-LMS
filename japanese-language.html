Training

What You Will Learn

The program balances exam preparation with real speaking practice, cultural awareness, and job-focused communication.

  • Reading, writing, listening, and speaking for beginner to pathway-ready learners.
  • JLPT/JFT exam preparation with vocabulary, grammar, and mock practice.
  • Workplace Japanese for caregiver, airport, construction, agriculture, and factory settings.
  • Interview practice, self-introduction, polite speech, and Japanese work culture.
