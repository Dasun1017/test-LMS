What You Will Learn

The Romanian program focuses on practical communication for students preparing for factory, warehouse, agriculture, and construction settings.

  • Basic Romanian pronunciation, greetings, numbers, and everyday phrases.
  • Workplace vocabulary for factory, warehouse, agriculture, and construction roles.
  • Safety instructions, supervisor communication, and daily job reporting phrases.
  • Employer interview practice and cultural orientation for working in Romania.
