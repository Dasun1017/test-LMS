About Us

About Confiance

Confiance Language & Vocational Training School (Pvt) Ltd is a professional language and vocational training institute based in Negombo, Sri Lanka.

Our Mission

Education that creates real opportunities

Our mission is to prepare students with the language skills, professional confidence, vocational knowledge, and career guidance needed to succeed in local and international employment markets.

Through high-quality teaching, practical training, cultural orientation, and international career guidance, we help Sri Lankan youth build brighter futures with confidence.

Founder Message

Yamuna Chandrika

It is with great pride and responsibility that I welcome you to Confiance Language & Vocational Training School (Pvt) Ltd.

My vision in establishing this institution was clear: to create real international career opportunities for Sri Lankan youth through quality language education and professional vocational training. Education is not only about gaining knowledge, but about building confidence, discipline, and the courage to step into the global workforce.

Vision

Leading training institution

To become a leading language and vocational training institution in Sri Lanka, recognized for developing skilled professionals for international career opportunities.

Mission
  • Provide high-quality language education and vocational training.

  • Promote discipline, confidence, and professional excellence.
  • Create ethical and transparent pathways for foreign employment.
Difference

What Makes Us Different?

A practical model that combines language learning, Sri Lankan classroom support, vocational preparation, and ethical guidance.

Native Teachers

Students learn accurate pronunciation, listening ability, and natural speaking confidence.

Sri Lankan Assistants

Every language class is supported by local teaching assistants who explain lessons clearly.

Practical Preparation

We focus on workplace vocabulary, interview skills, and cultural orientation.

Flexible Learning

Students can choose physical, online, or blended learning depending on schedule and location.

Pathway Model

Language skills, vocational knowledge, and career preparation are connected in one pathway.

Ethical Guidance
