What You Will Learn

The Italian program is practical and job-focused, with lessons built around real communication needs students face before and after travel.

  • Basic Italian grammar, pronunciation, and sentence building.
  • Daily conversation for travel, accommodation, shopping, and appointments.
  • Workplace vocabulary for hospitality, caregiving, agriculture, and construction.
  • Interview preparation, polite communication, and Italian work culture orientation.
