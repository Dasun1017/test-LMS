About Confiance

More than language classes

At Confiance, students are guided step by step from classroom learning to international career readiness through language, vocational skills, interview preparation, and transparent consultation.

  • Native teachers help students learn correct pronunciation, natural speaking patterns, and cultural communication.
  • Sri Lankan teaching assistants support every language program with clear explanations and additional classroom guidance.
  • Students can join physically at Negombo or attend online from anywhere in Sri Lanka.
  • Training is career-focused, with workplace vocabulary, cultural awareness, and interview readiness.
